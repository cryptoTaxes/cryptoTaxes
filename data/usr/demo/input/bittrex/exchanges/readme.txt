Place in this folder your BittrexOrderHistory_year.csv files, corresponding to the new
csv format used by Bittrex from 2018 onwards.

You can recognize this new format by checking the header which is:
  Uuid,Exchange,TimeStamp,OrderType,Limit,Quantity,QuantityRemaining,Commission,Price,PricePerUnit,IsConditional,Condition,ConditionTarget,ImmediateOrCancel,Closed

Notice also that these text files use UTF-8 text format.

They can be downloaded from your Bittrex account:
   Orders tab -> My Orders History -> Download History button.
